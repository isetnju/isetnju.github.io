About me

I received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University, in 2001. I am currently working as a full professor at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University. My current research is focused on natural language processing, software engineering, large language model, and image computing.
